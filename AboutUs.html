About Us

Twitter Trends gathers data from twitter and uses this data to rank the top 10 items you have from a category list. This is done by creating a category with a list of items in the form of a .csv format which should look like: item1, item2, item3 and so on. The ranking displayed is a simple count, based on the number of times your items hashtags has been used on twitter in the past 24hrs.
